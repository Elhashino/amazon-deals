About Sunblessed Savings

What We Do

We scan Amazon UK every single day to find the best deals and biggest discounts. Our automated system analyzes thousands of products to bring you only the deals worth your time.

No gimmicks, no fake deals - just genuine price drops verified by historical price data.

How We Find Deals

Our algorithm analyzes several key factors:

  • Price History: We track 90-day price trends to ensure discounts are real
  • Customer Reviews: Only products with 4+ star ratings and 100+ reviews
  • Sales Rank: We monitor popularity to find trending products
  • Deal Quality: Our scoring system ranks deals by value

Only deals meeting strict criteria make it to the site - typically 30% or more off with strong customer ratings.

How We Make Money

We're transparent about our business model:

When you purchase through our Amazon links, we earn a small commission (typically 1-10% depending on category). This costs you nothing extra - you pay the same price as going directly to Amazon.

These commissions help us keep the site free and continue scanning for deals daily. We only recommend products we genuinely believe are good deals.

  • No fake reviews or manipulated ratings
  • Clear affiliate disclosure
  • Your privacy protected (no selling of personal data)
